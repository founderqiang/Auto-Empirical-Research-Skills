# WRDS Research Database Quick Reference

## Pricing Data
- **CRSP**: US stock price, return, volume, market cap (daily/monthly)
- **TAQ**: High-frequency intraday trading data
- **Compustat Global**: International stock data
- **OptionMetrics**: Options prices, implied volatilities, Greeks (US/Europe)
- **Markit CDS**: Credit default swap data
- **TRACE**: US corporate bond prices and trading volume
- **Mergent FISD**: Bond characteristics and ratings
- **WRDS Bond Returns**: Pre-calculated bond returns (TRACE + FISD)
- **DealScan**: Commercial loan pricing and terms
- **Markit Securities Finance**: Short selling data

## Fundamental Data
- **Compustat NA/Global**: Balance sheet, income statement, cash flow (10-K, 10-Q)
- **Capital IQ**: Financial statements + key developments
- **WorldScope**: International fundamentals
- **Orbis (Bureau van Dijk)**: Global public AND private company data
- **WRDS Financial Ratio Suite**: Pre-calculated ratios at firm/industry level
- **Compustat Segments**: Geographic, industry, customer segment data
- **SDC**: M&A deals and new issues (IPO, SEO)
- **RavenPack**: News timestamps and headlines (macro + company-specific)

## Earnings and Estimates
- **I/B/E/S**: Analyst forecasts, guidance, actual earnings (US from 1975, intl from 1987)
- **Zacks**: Analyst forecasts, revisions, price targets, recommendations

## Institutional Ownership
- **Thomson-Reuters 13F**: Institutional holdings (S34, from 1980)
- **TR Global Ownership**: US 13F + non-US institutional data (from 1999)
- **FactSet 13F**: Institutional ownership (formerly LionShares)
- **WRDS SEC 13F**: Complete filings + amendments (from 2013)

## Fund Ownership
- **TR Mutual Fund (S12)**: Equity holdings quarterly (from 1980)
- **CRSP Mutual Fund**: Returns, NAV, expense ratios, loads (holdings from 2003)
- **MFLINKS**: Linking table between CRSP MFDB and TR

## Insider Ownership
- **TR Insider Data**: Stock and derivative transactions (from 1986)
- **WRDS-SEC Insider**: Complete filings + footnotes (from 2003)
- **Audit Analytics**: Shareholder activism data

## People and Governance
- **ExecuComp**: Executive compensation (from 1992)
- **BoardEx**: Officers and directors, networks, ownership (US/Global, from 1999)
- **ISS (RiskMetrics)**: Board member ownership, governance provisions (from 1996)
- **Capital IQ People Intelligence**: Executive and director profiles

## ESG and CSR
- **Sustainalytics**: ESG risk ratings
- **RepRisk**: ESG risk incidents
- **MSCI ESG KLD**: ESG ratings and controversies

## Key Linking Tables
- **CRSP-Compustat Merged**: Link stock data to fundamentals
- **MFLINKS**: Link mutual fund data across CRSP and TR
- **WRDS Linking Suite**: Cross-database identifiers
