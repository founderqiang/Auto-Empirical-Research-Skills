\noindent{\justifying{This table presents the association between local patent trading infrastructure and institutional ownership for a sample of Chinese A-share listed firms from 2003 to 2023. The dependent variable is \textsl{Institution}, defined as the ratio of shares held by institutional investors to total shares at year-end. The main explanatory variable is \textsl{PTC} (a dummy variable that equals one if the city where the firm is located has established a patent trading center in or before year $t$, and zero otherwise). All regressions include firm and year fixed effects. Province and City fixed effects are included as indicated in the table.
