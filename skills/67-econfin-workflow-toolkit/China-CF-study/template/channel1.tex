Column (4) evaluates the debt security margin via \textsl{IntCoverage} (the interest coverage ratio, calculated as the sum of total profit and interest expenses divided by interest expenses). All regressions incorporate firm, year, and city fixed effects. Robust $t$-statistics clustered by the firm are reported in parentheses.
